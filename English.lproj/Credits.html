Hide your desktop clutter on presentations

Debordelizer helps Finder to hide its desktop icons when you don't want to show then off. It can disable Notification Center temporarily, as well as dim a projector when you're making presentations with external display.

As a side effect, Finder needs to be restarted on some changes.
